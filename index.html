What We Offer.


Peace of Mind

When you go away you need to be confident that your cat is being well looked after, that's what we do best. We offer a flexible and reliable service so you can be sure your cat is looked after exactly how you wish, in the home they love.

We often have close bonds with our cats and it can be tough leaving them when we go away, that's why we provide you with the option to receive updates on your cats' wellbeing at no extra cost.

Along with regular updates, we offer services that are tailored to your needs, whether that be watering plants or drawing curtains, we can offer you more than just a happy cat on your return. If you would like to discuss with us what services we can provide whilst you're away then please feel free to ask us using the contact form at the bottom of the page.

Services

  • Emptying, cleaning and refilling of litter.
  • Topping up food and freshening water.
  • Playing and interaction.
  • Grooming.

  • Pre-sitting visit to discuss needs.

  • Updates while you're away.
  • Additional services can be requested.

What We Charge.


Fair Pricing

We believe that the cost of looking after your cat shouldn't be prohibitive. Because of this, we provide our services at a rate starting off at £6 a day for 1 cat.

Caring for more than one cat at a time costs slightly more as we feel each cat should receive a generous amount of attention in order to keep them happy and stop them feeling stressed. For each additional cat, the cost works out at roughly £2 a day extra.

We've provided a calculator for you to get an idea of what the cost will be, please be aware this is just a guide. You may want us to spend more or less time with your cat than our standard 30 minutes and hence this price may be adjusted accordingly, the same goes for additional services.

Why GCC.


We Love Our Job

The only way you end up doing any job for such a long time is by loving what you do. We love getting to meet new people, the satisfaction of them not having to worry whilst they're away and of course, we love cats.

Having relocated recently it's no longer possible to offer our service to our previous clients and so we're more determined than ever to prove ourselves as the best at what we do.

We've built our lives around looking after animals so far and have no plans to stop doing so, this means you can be confident we will be available when you need us.

Decades of Experience

Between us, we've got over two decades of experience in pet sitting having both started from a fairly young age and continued to do so being trusted by family, friends and clients.

This experience, particularly with cats, means that we're comfortable dealing with cats of all temperaments and know how to make them feel comfortable and relaxed in our company.

We understand that cats are often very particular in the way they do things, whether it be eating or sleeping, and we have the patience and experience to allow your cat to not be disrupted by suddenly having someone new enter their home.

Get In Touch.


Any Questions?

If you'd like to make an enquiry or booking then please contact us using the form provided. The more information you can provide the better, this means we'll be able to give you an accurate price quicker and discuss any additional needs for you as well.

Your cats mean a lot to you and so it's only right that you should have questions about how they are looked after while you're away. We aim to cover most questions in the sections above but understand that you may want to know more about how your cats will be cared for.

Where convenient for you we'll always make a visit to your home address before undertaking any care. We believe this is the ideal opportunity for you to make sure you're happy with the service we provide as well as making us aware of any requirements specific to you and your cats.
